Coco (2017)

Movie Summary

Coco is a movie about a young boy who wants to become a muscian. His family forbids his to follow his dreams so ends up stealing a guitar and adventuring through the after life on the day of the dead. This movie embraces Mexican culture, the importance of family, and shares the experience of dia de la muerte with the world.

Luca (2021)

Movie Summary

Luca is set on the Italian Riveria and is the story about a sea monster exploring life on land. He meets Alberto and sets on a crazy adventure to somehow obtain a Vespa. This movie embraces italian culture with the setting of seaside italy, italian architecture, pasta, fishing, and italian family dynamics. This movie also offers viewers a journey through discovering identity and searching for acceptance.

Turning Red (2022)

Movie Summary

Turning Red is about a young chinese-canadian girl, Mei, who must learn to tame her giant red panda and her emotions. This movie demonstrates the common struggles of seeking parental validation and learning to handle one's emotions. It shows asian culture through the girl's family, food, and common experiences growing up in her asian family. She navigates life, panda, and family to achieve her goal of attending the 4 town concert.
